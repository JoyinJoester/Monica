Monica Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

1. Google Drive Access Authorization

Monica accesses Google Drive only after you explicitly grant permission. Without your consent, the app will not connect to or read your Google Drive data.

2. Scope of Access

Monica only operates on the folder you select or files created by Monica for backup and sync purposes. It does not access unrelated files in your Drive.

3. Credentials and Account Security

Monica does not store your Google account password and does not ask for third-party account passwords. Authorization tokens are used only to perform actions you initiate, and you can revoke access at any time.

4. Data Usage and Sharing

Monica does not sell your data and does not share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only used for user-triggered functions such as backup, restore, and sync.

5. User Control

You can stop using the feature at any time, delete backup files from cloud storage, or revoke Monica's Google Drive access from your Google account permissions page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the project maintainer via Monica GitHub.

This page is intended for public policy disclosure and Google Drive integration review.
